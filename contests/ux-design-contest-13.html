UX Design Contest #13

Improve a food delivery app experience

👏 This contest ended May 17, 2020

🏆 Congratulations to Maryam and Najia Khan! 👏👏👏

Desired Outcomes

By participating in this contest, you will

  • Identify usability issues with a food delivery app experience (no purchase required)
  • Prioritize issues to address based on estimated impact and level of effort
  • Ideate to design 2-3 concept solutions
  • Test design concepts against the current experience with UsabilityHub
  • Create a super-powered portfolio case study using Notion
  • Practice working backwards to increase efficiency
  • Have a chance at winning cool prizes

How to Enter
Save time with Tools & Resources

  1. Pick a local food delivery app to improve (Examples)
  2. Identify usability issues with the app experience (no purchase required)
    • 🔎 Top Usability Issues Table
  3. Prioritize issues by estimated impact and level of effort to identify the top issue
  4. Design 2-3 solution concepts to fix the top issue
  5. Test your solutions against the current experience using UsabilityHub
  6. Document your project in a no-code case study using Notion or another online tool (e.g., Coda, Google Docs, Medium, etc.)
  7. Submit your entry by 11:59pm Pacific Time Sunday, May 17th.
