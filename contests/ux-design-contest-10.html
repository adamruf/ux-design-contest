Contest Rules

Anyone 18 years or older can participate on their own or as part of a team.

The deadline to submit your contest entry is Sunday, July 21, 2019 11:59PM PT.

Your project can be whatever you decide.

Only entries that meet all contest requirements will be eligible for winning a prize.

How to Enter

  1. Use UXPin to create an interactive prototype. If you're in it to win it, use as many of these as you can:
    1. Conditional Interactions
    2. Data
    3. Expressions
    4. Form Inputs
    5. States
    6. Variables
  2. Use Loom or Soapbox to record a 3-minute narrated presentation of your project.
  3. Use the Entry Form to submit links to your UXPin prototype and presentation video by July 21st.
