Contest Rules

Anyone 18 years or older can participate individually or with a group.

Entries must be original work created for this contest.

Your project can be whatever you decide.

Only entries that meet all contest requirements will be eligible for winning a prize.

The deadline to submit your entry is Saturday, February 29, 2020 11:59PM Pacific Time.

How to Enter

  1. Follow @UXDcontest on Twitter for contest updates
  2. Choose a website or app that you love to use
  3. Identify an important user experience to improve
  4. Pick 1-3 metrics you can use to measure success
  5. Design solutions to improve upon the current experience
  6. Test your solutions against the current experience using UsabilityHub
  7. Document your project in a no-code case study using Notion
  8. Submit your entry by 11:59pm Pacific Time Saturday, February 29th.
