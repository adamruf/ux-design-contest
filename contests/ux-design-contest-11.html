Contest Rules

Anyone 18 years or older can participate on their own or as part of a team.

The deadline to submit your contest entry is Sunday, August 18, 2019 11:59PM PT.

Only entries that meet all contest requirements will be eligible for winning a prize

How to Enter

  1. Identify a research goal for your study
  2. Design an online survey to collect quantitative and qualitative data
  3. Launch and promote your survey to collect at least 10 responses
  4. Analyze and synthesize your response data
  5. Create an online presentation of your research project and findings (no more than 10 slides)
  6. Subscribe to Adam's YouTube channel
  7. Submit your entry no later than August 18th at 11:59pm PT
