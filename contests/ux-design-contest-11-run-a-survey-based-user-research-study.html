Contest Rules

Anyone 18 years or older can participate on their own or as part of a team.

The deadline to submit your contest entry is Sunday, August 18, 2019 11:59PM PT.

Only entries that meet all contest requirements will be eligible for winning a prize

How to Enter

  1. Identify a compelling research goal
  2. Design an online survey to collect data for your study
  3. Launch and promote your survey to collect as many responses as you can
  4. Analyze and synthesize your response data
  5. Communicate your findings in a presentation (no more than 10 slides)
  6. Submit your entry no later than August 18th at 11:59pm PT
