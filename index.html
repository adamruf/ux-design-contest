What's UX Design Contest?

UX Design Contest is a fun way for designers to

  • show the world what they can do
  • exercise UX, UI, and Product design skills
  • learn new skills and tools
  • grow their design portfolio
  • win impactful prizes

After reviewing hundreds of UX design portfolios, Product Designer + Design Mentor, Adam Ruf, created UX Design Contest to provide useful and structured projects for exercising full-stack design skills and growing your design portfolio.

How does UX Design Contest work?

  1. UX Design Contest theme is decided
  2. Official contest details are posted
  3. Watch Product Designer, Adam Ruf, on YouTube for live interactive design sessions + contest support
  4. Follow the requirements and submit your entry by the deadline
  5. Entries are reviewed and scored
  6. Participants receive feedback on their entries and winners are announced live on Adam's weekly live YouTube stream.
  7. Winners will be listed on this website
