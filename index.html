How does UX Design Contest work?

  1. UX Design Contest theme is announced
  2. Participants collaborate on a doc to define contest details
  3. Official contest details are posted
  4. Follow the requirements and submit your entry by the deadline
  5. Your entry will be reviewed live on stream
  6. Contest winner will be announced live on stream
  7. Winning entries will be showcased on this site

If you've got questions or need some inspiration, check out ux_adam's Twitch channel as he tackles the challenge live on Twitch
